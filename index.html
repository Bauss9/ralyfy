The Big Idea

Transforming how non-planners organize their events

ralyfy is an AI-powered, all-in-one event planning platform designed for non-planners - parents, teens, executives, and anyone who hates the stress of planning events. It solves universal pain points like decision overload, group chaos, budget blowouts, and time suck, making planning fast, fun, and viral.

The platform transforms the planning process by allowing users to answer just 5 simple questions or use voice commands, then AI does the rest - suggesting venues, catering, decor, and managing the guest list - all while keeping everything on budget.

Whether it's birthdays, weddings, corporate retreats, or game nights, ralyfy handles everything from ideation to execution, so users can focus on enjoying their events, not stressing about planning them.

User Flow Example

Follow Chad as he plans his 30th birthday party

1

Quick Setup

Chad opens ralyfy and taps "New Event." He answers 5 questions: "Birthday," "20 guests," "$1,000 budget," "Near me," and "Casual vibe." The AI generates an initial plan.

2

Budget Tweaking

The AI suggests a "Rooftop Bar" ($800) and "Taco Truck" ($400), but that's over budget. Chad says, "Hey ralyfy, find a cheaper venue," and it suggests "Community Hall" ($450), bringing him under budget.

3

Group Coordination

Chad invites Sarah and Mike as co-planners. They vote on food options, and tacos win with 75% approval. Chad sends RSVPs, and 15 of 20 people confirm attendance.

4

Finalizing Details

Chad locks in the plan, earning the "Venue Master" badge. He books the venue and catering through ralyfy's vendor marketplace, securing everything with just a few taps.

5

Sharing Success

After the party, ralyfy creates a digital scrapbook with photos and guest messages. Chad shares it on Instagram: "Chad's 30th—planned with ralyfy in just 10 minutes!" His friends are impressed and download the app.
